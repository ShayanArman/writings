On Creation
poetry

a guide:

the idea that we know everything,

from the beginning,

is patently false.

We write some code,

and then the next step reveals itself.

It is more a process of creation,

rather than a moment.

Similar to how the universe was created,

a grain of sand, then two, then the next.

Or a human being,

from one cell, to the next, to organs, to fully formed human;

walking around, causing absolute havoc, and writing some poetry along the way.
