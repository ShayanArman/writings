Very truly, what is the problem?

If not in the body

Usually

The problem is in the mind

In its attempt to solve problems

It creates a problem!

Too much thinking is in itself a painful state

Sometimes

The problem is in the body

But even then

The mind can create recurrence

Not letting it properly heal

And very occasionally

The problem is real

Not of the mind

But in the environment or the body

Sometimes

The pain that we feel is not what it seems

But a lack of something more than a signal of anything

You see the downside of the upside

Is there lack thereof

Can create a pain signal too

So getting hyped up all the time on the new

Only ups and ups your level

When the high goes away

The pain signal of a lack grows more severe

So where is the problem?
