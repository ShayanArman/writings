Andrew Tate
On Infamy

I was partying in London, England one night with these Frenchies I met outside a bar.

We were getting a few beers, everybody was in a good mood.

“Where you guys from?”

FRANCE

“Why are you here?”

Our friend here is on Exchange. We’re visiting him.

“THATS AWESOME I LOVE EXCHANGE”

Ya it’s been incredible!

We went Bar hopping. Drink after drink. I was still rich so it was quite fun.

I wasn’t worried about money, especially because I found the deal of a lifetime at this hotel next to Paddington. I hopped in a cab with this Russian guy that almost got me killed, so whatever right? Saved 7 pound baby. Another beer.

We’re at this other bar in SOHO, when they start talking about this Famous Tik Toker.

“You don’t know who Andrew Tate is?”

No. - I said

“Okay he’s this guy on Tik Tok that got super famous. He’s EVERYWHERE”

Oh shit haha

“Ya. So he’ll say something right. And every word you’re like ya. Ya. Ya. And then in the end, when you really think about it, you don’t agree at all! But he speaks so well you know?”

That’s hilarious. So do girls really like the French accent?

“At this bar a few weeks ago I met Jeremy Clarkson’s daughter.”

No way.

“Ya I think they can hear it for sure and she came up to me. Crazy night.”

Later I went home to Canada, after partying more that night at a different viby club. I loved London. Anyways I go back to Canada, and I remembered what these kids said. “The most famous man right now.”

So I looked him up! Who wouldn’t. And I realized something only years later…
