True Friend

The only ones who want nothing from you,

but your existence,

are the True Friends and Family.

But to get to that level…

where you want Nothing from them,

is hard too.

You must let go of your desire,

to want anything from anyone in a Transactional way,

To be a True Friend.

That takes work…

It helps to not want much in General,

and that way, your relationships won’t be fake,

But real.
