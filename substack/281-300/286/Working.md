Post Script.

I realized there’s no “Man”. We all work for somebody in a Death Loop.

The labourer works for the Foreman.

The Manager works for the General Manager

The GM works for Head Office

Head Office works for the CEO.

The CEO might be the Founder but he works for the Investors.

The investors work for shareholders.

The shareholders work for their own investors and customers.

The Customers work for the Government.

And the Government works for the Corrupt Politicians.

And the Corrupt Politicians…. they work for the Army.

This keeps going… we all work for somebody.
