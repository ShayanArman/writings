On Marx

My Brain is a Rumination Machine.

Its crazy. Something will come up. A thought.

And then a week later. Maybe a month.

I’ll ask the same Question.

And the Answer is all of a Sudden with Me.

Like a Friend I have *yet to meet.

I wait for him, and then boom, the friend is at my door, waiting for Tea and Biscuits.

I asked a Question months ago.

If all the Land is not owned by Capitalists,

but instead by the people,

how do you divide it?

The ones by the water are obviously hot commodity.

So who gets to live there in a non-capitalist system?

Well firstly, nobody can ‘own’ the land. Otherwise how would you divvy it up?

So in non-capitalist systems there is no land ownership.

By anybody.

Its usage based.

So then who gets to use it?

“To each according to his needs.” is the passage.
