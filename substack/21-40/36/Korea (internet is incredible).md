Korea (internet is incredible)
Philosophy takes time (like everything)

I arrived in Korea today, and began walking.

I soon arrived in a quaint neighbourhood, near the American base.

Most people spoke a bit of English, and a diverse crowd was around.

Pretty soon I realized the tech here is … lightning fast. Everything is fast.

By the time you’ve ordered, and put your card in the reader, everything is pretty much done.

It’s scary.

If you have a shopping addiction, this is the place for you.

By the time you get triggered to buy, its already done. Fast.

Internet is everywhere. I just uploaded 3 videos to YouTube, wrote a LinkedIn post, made a Substack post, in under 10 minutes. WHAT.

Philosophize that ladies and gentleman.

I always thought there wasn’t enough time for Philosophy. But in Korea, there’s time for everything.
