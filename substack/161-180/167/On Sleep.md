On Sleep

When i was in my early twenties

I had trouble sleeping

I tried everything

Nasal surgeries

Sprays

Breathe right strips

Everything

Better pillow. I even spent 100$ on a pillow once. My friends came over and begged me to try it. Nobody had ever heard of someone spending 100$ on a pillow before.

Better mattress 5000$.

Drugs

Cbd

Really anything

Then one day i kinda just slept

Like really slept without any issue

I threw the drugs away

And the slumber continued

Then i got into napping

And pretty soon i was sleeping even in the afternoons

Then i got into dreaming

And i would interpret my dreams

One time i dreamt i was holding all the right cards

I interpreted this as That everything was coming together

Another week i was dreaming constantly of being surrounded by famous people

And i realized that fame would soon come to me

My dreams became more real

My afternoon sleeps became deeper

And i would have visions

Deep understanding of my life

My soul came to the forefront

How could there not be a soul? Id wake up wondering in a daze

And what if God is real? Id tremble in fear, knowing i had to live the Good life

What started out as a problem

Soon became a blessing

Id sleep once and work

Then id sleep again in the afternoon wake up and finish off what i started in the morning

Coding became easier

Any problems i encountered id soon sleep and they’d be solved

I discovered you can nap together with people

So id go back to iran and in the afternoon after our lunches my cousins and i would take a quick 2 hour nap on the floor

I began to love sleep

Flying around the world id plan around my sleep schedule

A late night flight was usually cheaper. Id get a free hotel as well since i could sleep anywhere.

Bus rides? Time for a quick recharge.

At this point id mastered sleep.

I had this special sleep playlist on both spotify and apple music downloaded.

Id play it and i knew it was time.

Sleep would overwhelm my eyes.

My body would slow down, get heavy, and boom.

(Funny in many cultures we say he’s a Heavy sleeper, as I’ve discovered language holds the key to understanding)

Gone.

Sleep became so automatic. So full. It was as if id pressed the fast forward button.

Unawares id wake up with two hours or an hour gone.

I almost missed the days of restless sleep.

This was getting scary.

How could time go by so fast?

I became so unaware of my sleep, so deep and restful, i began to question my waking hours.

Are they passing by just the same? I wondered.

I get on a train, and the destination arrives.

May it never come!

May we stay asleep forever.
