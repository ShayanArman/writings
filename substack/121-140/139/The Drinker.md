The Drinker
And Circular Dependencies

If what you are going through,

Requires a State Change,

The drink will give you that. For sure.

Right away.

But the State Change that occurs after the drink

the next day, is the issue.

Now you’re in a circular dependency…

Be careful!

You have more at stake than just your liver.

Your soul!

You’re only here once!

Do it for you!

Put it down.

And wait. Rejuvenate.
And you will feel better I promise.

If it has to do with other things, isn’t it better to have energy to sort them out?
Rather than seek the Curse?

Which will make you far worse in the long run?
