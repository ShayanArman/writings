Dying Twice

Possibly the most important thing to be done,

on your Todo List put it at Number One,

Is to not die Once,

But take a Chance,

and Die One More,

Earlier than Before,

To learn the Value of Forgiveness,

and Deliverance,

Letting Go,

To become a Bit More than Before,

To lose all Fear of The Deep,

And take a Dive into the Unknown,

Falling into the Cosmos,

Being taken away,

Like a climber falling off a Crag,

Being caught by a Friend,

In that moment of the Fall,

Finally feeling Free,

For a Split Second,

Knowing there is no more to do,

No more to fight for,

Nothing can be done,

Letting Go.

The most beautiful is a Man who hates no one,

And the most vile is a Man who has been Hurt by Others,

and begins to exchange the Favour.

Turn the Other Cheek,

Is not to allow for Harm,

But to not allow the Harm onto yourself…
