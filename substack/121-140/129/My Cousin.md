My Cousin

I have a cousin who messages me every morning.

He says horrible shit: “Shayan did you just hear about this kid dying in a Fire in Spain?”

No I answer. I didn’t know that. So sad…

Then the next day: “Shayan, did you hear!?”

What I ask?

“People are starving in this war zone. Its horrible man.”

I’m so sorry to hear that…

My gut wrenches.

“Shayan what’s up!?”

Oh god I think. Here we go again as I clench my stomach…

You wouldn’t listen to this cousin for too long would you?

How long would you respond to his texts?

Then why do you watch the news?
