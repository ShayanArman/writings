and Money Laundering

I once saw a picture of this Beautiful West Van home,

and I told my cousin: “Buy it for us.”

I imagined partying in it. Inviting everyone over and actually having a real reason to drink and celebrate.

And his wife said winged words: “Why don’t you buy it for us?”

I’ve said this before, that you’d be surprised the difference between people..

Other than Conceptual Spiritual.

Okay okay. I know that’s a big one.

But really, what are you, but a Concept?

If you were to describe yourself, no one says: “I’m 5ft 11.5, I have biceps of 5 inches, my eye colour is Blue…” No of course not! You say: “I was born here, My family is this, I am nice, I am beautiful, hard working, intelligent.”

Bruv. You think you’re the only hard working intelligent person out there?

In my view, you’re the same as Joe Shmo.

And you’re out there asking Joe Shmo for money!

But there’s absolutely no difference between you two.

Next time someone comes and asks for cash, ask them for cash.

They will look at you like you’re nuts!

But you both are. For not knowing the Truth.

That we are Conceptual.

We are Equal.
