Belief Systems
and the Don Reawakened

My last post on Trust Fund Babies highlighted the need to diminish the Value of others through attributing false gains and false means to their success.

This lowers the need for us to do anything, and especially to listen to anyone but ourselves.

In effect, this is actually a tale of the Don. Where we must believe in the Process, before any outcome can actually be gained.

What we do to remain in our place, to not change, and not listen is to:

devalue / falsify the process (he didn’t make the money his dad gave it to him)

davalue  / falsify the outcomes (he’s not that rich)
