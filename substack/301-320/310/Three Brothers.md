Three Brothers

One brother goes and meets his two brothers who are standing there. He says, “Brothers, I have something to show you.” They ask, “What is it?” And he replies, “It’s incredible, beyond your wildest imagination. I want to take you both there so you can see.”

So, they begin walking, and they encounter many obstacles. They continue to walk, and many other things happen along the way.
