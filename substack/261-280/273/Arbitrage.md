“I tell you Man, that I am Rich here, but Poor there!”

‘Gosh friend. You are a lucky Man!’

“And why do you say that?”

‘Because I am Poor here. And Poor there!’

“But you don’t listen to my words!”

‘Well go on then! Teach me how I can be Rich Some-Where!’

“That is the Key is it not? To be Rich Some Where! Is it not better than No Where?”

‘Ah I think I see what you mean… It is perfectly fine to be Poor Here. As long as we are Rich There!’

“Yes. If you need to borrow here. And do whatever it takes. Even to be Poor Here. But Rich There. Is there a difference?”

‘No. Because one needs to be Rich Somewhere!’

“And is it not True that Riches bring Riches?”

‘Why that’s what They Say!’

“So if you are Rich There. And Poor Here. And use your Riches There, to create more Riches, you can pay off your Debts here!”

‘I’m following’

“And then pretty soon. You will be Rich There. And Rich Here. And that is the Wealth of Nations.”

‘So wealth must come from Some Where!’

“It must come from Somewhere. And is that not how God made the Universe?”

‘Did he do it on Loan?’

“Something cannot come from Nothing. I say that God is a Gambler. He borrowed There, and Gave it Here!”
