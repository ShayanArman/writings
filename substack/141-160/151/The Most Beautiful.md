The Most Beautiful

Money is so powerful,

It will make a Poor man Rich!

Money is so wonderful,

It will make a Bald man Beautiful!

Money is so Great,

It will turn a dummy into a Philosopher,

Money is so neat,

It will clean up a pig stye, forgive sins, and even bring people back to life!

Money solves problems!

I once wrote down 20 of my problems, and realized 19 of them could be solved by money!

So I only ever have 2 problems.

One of soul,

and one of body,

Money will solve the latter,

and calm the former,

But money is also evil.

It can do too much!

Filling the heart with too much passion.

It can drive a man crazy! When he realizes what he can do with it.

He might even forget he is just another Man, with his nice clothes and beautiful cars.

His confidence might go haywire! Sleeping with Lions. Driving fast cars.

There are places in this world, running over a Man, you must pay his family members a ransom.

An eye for an eye becomes treasure for an eye.

But it can make us forget a few things too.

There are people that sold their soul for money!

But are there people who sold their money for their soul?
