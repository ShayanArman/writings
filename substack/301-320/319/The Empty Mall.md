The Empty Mall

Proponents of Capitalism will state that it Produces and Abundance of Goods and Services

But anyone with eyes can see it isnt the size of the grocery store that matters

But the Ratio to your Wallet

You can be in a sparsely filled Mall

With a fat wallet

And will have more fun

Then a Filled to the Brim bazaar

With an empty Wallet

You see it isnt the total Output of the Economy

But it in Ratio to your own Output within that Economy

Same with everything.
