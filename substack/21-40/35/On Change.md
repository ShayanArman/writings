On Change
and its location

No man is perfect.

So then change is always necessary.

If Change is necessary, then where does it occur?

What is its location?

Time wise, it always has to occur in the now.

Of course, there are environments and conditions, in which Change is easier to accomplish.

However, that still occurs in the present moment.

I.e whatever it is you are dealing with, internally, or externally, that needs attention and energy, must be dealt with.

There is no escaping it.

We might say: “i will wait until this condition is correct.”

However, even then, at some point, you will come face to face with what needs doing!

Now usually, Change is painful. It might be that way because the gears of the mind are set towards consistency . Explained previously as a great survival mechanism - what worked before, will work now, unless something changes externally or internally to throw a previous conclusion in doubt.

Therefore, if Change was easily done, the work of the past would be… undone

To Change then is a task that is in many ways delicate.

Be careful pulling out the weeds; you don’t know what they were holding in place.
