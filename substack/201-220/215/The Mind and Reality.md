The Mind and Reality
And Danish Girls in Byron Bay

I’m about to do it again… hold me back. Philosophy is about to come out of me like a Demon about to be removed from a Cage…

I’m a good Coder, a great Logician, an incredible designer, an okay entrepreneur, an incredible promoter, and a terrible boyfriend.

But what I am… what I am… is simply the most exceptional Modern Philosopher this world has…

The Mind my friends, is a Reflection.

I was walking around Byron Bay, and I see this girl right? She was stunning.

Words cannot describe. I won’t but I’ll just say she was traveling from Denmark.
Anyways, months later I’m sitting at a coffee shop and I realize, damn I think she really liked me.

I mean how sad is that. It’s early September. Cold already. I’m sitting outside. Distracted from coding. And all I can think about is that Danish girl was stunning, and she was into ME?!

But then I realized something…

The mind…

Is a REFLECTION.
