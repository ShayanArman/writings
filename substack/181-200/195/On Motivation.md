So let’s get into it then, On Motivation and its exact definition.

All human excitement is through Change.

If something was Good (not harmful) 5 minutes ago, and it has not changed

Then it is therefore not harmful now.

Simply put, we are looking for change in the environment, both external and internal.

So then what is motivation?

Motivation is the highest slope of Change that occurs. The slope of change, either in excitement, brain chemistry, is not the absolute change. Not the muscles growing. Not the bones getting denser. Not the mind being sharper. No none of that. It is the slope of change. The acceleration. That is what we feel.

So then what is motivation?

The exact moment (almost always at the point of decision), when we *feel the largest change in our mind. That moment is at the beginning of the Path or Process. When we decide to change. That decision to change, produces the Greatest Change we will ever feel in the process of Change.

Which is why everybody gives up.

That first moment has the most heightened feelings. Since it has the greatest SLOPE of Change. From being a Fat Loser sitting on your couch. To “I’m gonna be the Greatest!”

That moment is the single most Accelerant moment of Change in Mind. At the beginning…
