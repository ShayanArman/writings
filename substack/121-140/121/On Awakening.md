On Awakening

In the literal sense, I woke up this morning, and noticed the Coffee Shop

that was so empty last night…

Was full again!

The Streets empty and desolate,

Were buzzing!

I noticed this movement, over and over, and I thought:

“What drives this movement? Every single one of these people woke up and Sprung right back up again!”

And I asked if this same internal drive, was what drove the Universe, Galaxies, Planets, everything to Move….

And that the opposite of Movement was Stillness

To Sit despite the Drive to Move, and bring Awareness to what the fuck is going on.

Perhaps Stillness is the opposite of this movement, and it is needed to make a new Move, to decide ‘what’s next!’ in a better way.

Then meditation in a way goes against the ‘urge’ or the ‘scurge’

Again, for most of you that live a delectable life full of joy and Love, going with the Flow is perfectly fine. Why would you resist?

But to resist as someone who wishes to bring on change, ask the Universe for a difference, is that not something Honorable?

Resistance however is futile. As the Force, the Driver, is so much more powerful than anything we can imagine.

Would you fight a current head on? A Tsunami with sticks?

So then the Way is to Accept, to Observe, and in the act of Observing, to allow change to come naturally.

For not only is the Drive Powerful beyond reckoning, it has a wily way of changing. Nothing stands still as they say.

So to simply observe the Movement, Change will come naturally… whether we like it or not.
