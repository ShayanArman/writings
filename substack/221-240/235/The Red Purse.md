The Red Purse

You know the purse wasn’t red when he bought it for me.

And how did it get the beautiful red tinge it has now?

When I found out how he made his money. It just turned.

And how did he make his money?

He only told me once. I listened. Then the purse changed.

Yes but how did he make it?

On a yacht somewhere in the Mediterranean.

Could you enlighten us the Color of the Yacht?

Come to think of it the bathrooms were all Red.

And his Car?

Red as well. All Red.

And you care not?

About what?

The Red. Why everything he owns is Red.

Not really. Come to think about it, this is the first time I’ve really talked about it.

Maybe you’re the Painter?

You mean I painted everything Red?

Well things only turned Red after you showed up. So maybe the Reason is the Cause?

Never held a paint brush in my life.

You don’t need a brush to Paint what he has Painted.
