Its Easy

It was hard to find… and easy to lose

So difficult was my path, so easy to be led astray

Impossible to create, so easy to destroy

I was once told to always hold my head up high, no matter the circumstance.

Whoever wrote that, has never been beaten down by the hands of … well themselves. For fate is a mirror of our own choices.

It does not hurt us, to be confident. Not over confident, but at least have a good sense of self. Despite the external circumstance, because we don’t control everything.
