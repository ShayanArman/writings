On Suffering

You know,

Jesus could have played the Victim,

and said: “boo meee, all i wanted to do was show the people the Truth”

and said: “but they killed me for it! for the love of mankind?”

and said: “my morality was dope! my logic sound. and yet here I am sentenced to death”

No, he was the ultimate Gangster. And said let me use this to spread my message MORE.

To show them I believe the Truth so hard. So steadfast. I will happily walk to the Other Side.

Ultimate Gangster.

To believe so strongly in the Truth.

Man he believed in the Truth, as much as you believe in your paycheque coming in two weeks.

That’s how hard this guy believed!

He believed in the Truth,

as hard as you believe tomorrow will be Light,

and you will alight with it!

That’s how hard he believed in the Truth.

He might have believed it even harder!

Like how you look at your hands, and say: “There they are!”

That’s how hard he saw his Truth.

So then did he Suffer? Or was the Pain the necessary ingredient for such miraculous feats. As to have a non-christian 2000 years later writing about him?

Perhaps Glory is the Greatest of Paths.

Pleasure only goes so far. Love goes further. And Glory is everlasting.
