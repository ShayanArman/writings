Ask God Not
for your Measly Dreams

<todo-image-shayan: add image `How could I have known, the life I’d live?`>

Never ask God for your dreams

Because you dreamed them

You might accidentally believe you did it

And mistake yourself for the great power of God

So ask for what Spirit can show you

So above and beyond your measly imagination

So you can prove once and for all

To everyone gazing

To everyone wistfully asleep

To everyone who doubted Him

He is real

He is all powerful

He will show you how Great He is
