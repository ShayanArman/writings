Globalism
and the Destruction of Culture

Adherence to Globalism,

without Respect for Culture,

Ruins and absolutely destroys the very Cultures

that they Virtue Signal their love and desire for.

Because Globalism goes hand in hand with Capitalism,

and that driver,

only knows one destination,

profit, at the expense of culture.

Unless of course,

it can form a K-Pop group and sell that culture.

If not. Drink that latte, buy those nikes, and watch that Netflix.

Por Favor.
