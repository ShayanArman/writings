To Be

Many people on Earth

I bet if you were to ask

Choose to be anything

They would answer

“Rich”

Now they most likely would never say

“Mega Rich” which is the actual right answer to that question

Because ive already been rich.

And seriously apart from some cool vacations

It onky enhances what you already are

Yes you taste and experience new things that money can buy.

But you dont necessarily go further than that.

So it bought me access to some incredible places. And i got to meet cool people.

But it didnt change my entirely view on Reality.

I was still me.

Now being the 10000 or so billionaires.

Those guys have to literally change everything.

Imagine getting phone calls all day people asking for stuff.

That would be pretty lonely.

Honestly. Now that i think about it. Im happy im still me. And intact. That level of wealth would have absolutely screwed me up beyond fixing.
