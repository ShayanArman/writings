With Warren Buffett leaving Apple,

A good one is the idea of limiting our actions in the market,

to a few important ones we sit on for a lifetime.

You might say: "Well of course, if you could find 5 1000x returns, then you don't need to do much! But how does one do so? That is the hard part!"

But contained within the strategy is the wisdom. Like a biblical parable of old, you have to look deeper.

*By focusing on only a few 'actions', and knowing we only have a few actions, we then dive deeper, and are *more likely to find those big winners!

Patience and due diligence not only allow us to wait until we see something truly good. It also enables us to not make too many mistakes that could sink us!
