“Production, saving, efficiency, and return on equity”

Nature was the main producer, and now we use nature to produce.

Output increases over time with less input as efficiency increases.

Those increase lead to higher savings.

Those savings are then put into other machines that can then produce as well, continuing the growth cycle.
