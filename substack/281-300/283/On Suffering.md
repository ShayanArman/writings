On Suffering

Man, is there no difference

Between Real hardship

And the Ruminations of the Mind

Can one exist without the other?

For if there is no Mind given

Is the Real even Hard?

I suppose you could say

Hardship cannot exist without Mind

But the Event can exist without Attention

But you see,

Attention can be turned off in an Instant

Imagine in Sleep

Or Hallucination

Or Death

But the Event cannot be simply turned Off

So is one Worse?

Perhaps it is the Question itself that is Skewed.

Hardship cannot exist without Mind

Because it is a Creation of the Mind

But an Event can exist without Mind

At that point it is not Hardship

But simply an occurrence

Being without Pain

Just Is

So is one worse?

Physical Hunger cannot be turned off Simply by wishing it so

A lack is a lack

But Rumination can be changed in an instant

So perhaps then, Real Pain in the senses is by far Worse than Mind Pain

When experienced

Because you cannot just click the Off Switch

On debt

Good knows I’ve tried
