Reverse Inference

On training the mind through breath

Imaging training an ai model on knowledge it always has

That wouldn’t do too great

Unless of course you are to change the actual schema of the ai model

Its initial parameters

Without going so far

Training only on the same data

You won’t improve too much!

Same with the mind

Since all is experienced through the mind

And our thoughts are the number one sound

Listen even if you are in a new place listening to a symphony

Your thoughts are still running

Listening to the Breathe

We allow new data to enter

Focusing on the Breathe

We allow ourselves to truly change

We allow new data in

We open ourselves up to

Change.
