Its Easy Shy!

The Life of a Poor Man, is quite hilarious.

I mean firstly, to be Unknown AND on top of that to be Broke,

(Despite your own making of such a conundrum)

is nevertheless quite hilarious.

To achieve anything in life, you have to count on others to an extent.

Some help, is absolutely needed. Not always in the form of transactions “money, housing, fuel, etc.”, but simply in the form of emotional support and connection.

This is again as it shows up in every single endeavor, an example of the Cold Start problem…

You see Manifestation is actually a form of the Cold start problem.

One does not start an engine without any energetic input.

It must come from somewhere!

The positive energy needed to convince others to follow you, when you are broke as a joke, cannot possibly well up from inside of a Black Hole…

It must be generated from somewhere…

That generation can either come from the Past, (already within us, given in a past time),

or Outside of Us, in the form of God or Spirit.

This spiritual “Swelling Up Inside” combats the effects of Poverty,

to Jigger a Swelling of Joy, enough until the External Matches the internal,

At that point, … its Easy.

The Cold Start Problem is one of the most difficult problems.

Past that, we encounter deadly competition with other Engines already running.

That becomes Game Theory, and Competition.

Most people never encounter that level of Competition. Because they are too busy barely surviving…
