Inverted Living

It was on the 20th day of straight partying

That my friend ashley at the hostel said

“Just stay in tonight. Well watch a movie.”

I wish id listened to her winged words

Words filled with a Quiet song of Wisdom

but instead i continued the rager

A rolling stone gathers no Moss right?

On that night i noticed my body recoiling

My muscles getting tighter

I knew deep down something was seriously wrong..

That was the only night of the 30 days of straight partying on the Greek islands something seriously went wrong

Like really bad wrong

Like i wish i just watched that movie bad

But this series on the Mind is incredible

As the tension built up

I could sense something would happen

Now did i foretell an Occurrence?

We will never know the cause for sure

But humour me and let us assume on circumstantial evidence that the internal Tightness and Tension in fact did make the night out go Wrong… oh so Wrong.

The logical mind would say if this is so.

And God cares for both Yin and Yang

And the Dark is as equal as the Light in producing effects

Perhaps then the feelings of Goodness, Health, and Lightness produce the complete opposite effects?

And how can we control this, but simply to let go. To heal. And allow the Tension to slip away and the Good to come Up to being?
