Double Taxation
the Party

Some things are positive reinforcers → I eat good, I perform well, work longer, and sleep better, and therefore work better.

Some things are negative reinforcers → I partied last night, and now I’m tired so I can’t work.

So I lose money from lost production.

I lost money last night because I overspent on the party.

Therefore, it is a double tax to party.

Sometimes though, if done well, you can feel rejuvinated and invogorated from a Good night with Pals, and it is then the opposite effect.
How you party dictates many things in your life.

Choose wisely.
