To Serve

All business are Services (even Product ones)

They Serve the customer

How well they do this, how often they fulfill their promises,

is the quality of the Brand and Business.

Iterating quickly, understanding the user, and making adjustments in “features” and “updates” is the hallmark of a good Business.

Further announcing these updates allows the business to capture headlines,

continuing the Brand image of ‘cares for the end user’. And ‘enhancing’ the mind of its customers through this process of loyalty to the customer.
