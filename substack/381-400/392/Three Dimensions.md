The Hunter and his Gimpy Friend

Gimpy! HAHAHAHHAAHA GIMPY! HAHAHAHA

“Ya okay. Okay laugh. Yup. SO funny. It’s easy to laugh they call you Mr. Awesome. Mr. Incredible.”

Sorry you know I don’t laugh when you’re not around. It’s just funny. How do they come up with this stuff?

“Its honestly depressing. I gimp because I fell. So now they call me Gimpy in the tribe.”

Everyone has a name in the tribe. We’re bored. So we make fun of each other.

“No one makes fun of you.”

Because I’m never there! I’m out hunting! You hang out long enough obviously they’ll make fun of you. You just chill with the women. It’s weird!

“Okay I get it but I can’t go hunting. You know that.”

Ya sorry to bring it up again. It’s just funny. I waited a while to laugh like that. I couldn’t laugh behind your back. You know that.

“Let’s move on. I have a question for you.”

“They say you’re so famous now. Its not just the tribe. They say the neighboring tribes are starting to talk about you.”

Ya I reached the second dimension a while back.

“And what was the first?”

Well the first dimension was knowing myself. It took time. I went out hunting alone in the dark. I grew my own senses. I spent days out there. Eventually I grew accustomed to my own smell.

“What’s the second?”

What you just said. The other tribes started talking about me.

“Is there a third?”

I knew you’d ask that. Ya there is.

“What is it?”

The third is across time. They will speak of me a thousand years into the future.
