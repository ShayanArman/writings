On Vitamin C

So there are actually times of course where it makes sense for a Man to care about Public Policy.

I only see two such cases.

1) where his way of life, cultural values are or will be destroyed

2) where the public policy is so skewed that he can no longer give attention to his Private life

If either of these cases are true, he has two choices personally

1) leave

2) stay and work through whatever needs to be done

Now most people care about the former, so they are constantly in uproar. I find this to be true in non-mono-ethnic or singular culture environments.

Shocker. Youve got a whole bunch of different views and people who have no lives constantly bickering.

This is not at all useful or constructive, as you have two sides constantly yelling how they feel.

This produces nothing. And there is no endgame.

Its like us sitting across the table, and i yell how much i love apples and the virtues of apples.

And you retort how tasty oranges are, and that vitamin c is a wonderful chemical.
