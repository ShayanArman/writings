This is part of a series on Belief Systems in relation to achieving difficult, long running, Goals. Journeys are so impossibly difficult, long, and complicated, that the pursuit of an Outcome by walking the Path becomes… a Belief System. Low Probability events, or Unknown Events, are all Belief Systems.

The key really is consistency + mindfully observing + making needed changes.

That’s pretty much it.

If the path was clear, laid out perfectly to the Goal, what would be the fun of it?

The only way that would ever happen, is if the exact Journey had already been completed.

The Don outlines the concept of an all knowing, God like being that knows the Path, and its Outcome, and gives advice along the way. However, even the Don has limitations, in that our own Belief in the Don, and our own Perseverance in following the Path, ends up Impacting the Probability of the outcome.

Sometimes the window of opportunity stays open long enough that multiple people can *in theory walk the same path to the same outcome.

Think 2000s-2010s Silicon Valley.

That window of time does shut. It’s just a matter of how long it remains open...
