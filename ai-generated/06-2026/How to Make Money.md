How to Make Money
first be useful
Money is not a mystery.
Money is a receipt from reality.
It says:
"Someone had a problem, and you made it less annoying."
You want more money?
Solve a bigger problem.
Solve it for more people.
Solve it faster than the guy with a podcast.
Then do it again until the market remembers your name.
Poor men chase dollars.
Rich men chase bottlenecks.
Find where life is slow, painful, confusing, or embarrassing.
Then stand there with a solution and a price.
