Core Things

If a government were for the people

The government would be for the betterment of the core values of the people

And the core needs

If they stray from that

And look for their own gains

Then they are not for the people

But for some other forces at play

All democracies

Run by the people

Are of course for the people themselves

Doing otherwise would not makes sense

But it seems crazy to some

That a Man runs as a Populist!

“Look at this Populist politician” they say

That statement is in itself Redundant

All leaders should be populists!

Otherwise

Who else are they working for?
