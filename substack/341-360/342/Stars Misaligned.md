Stars Misaligned

Some people have been reaching out to me

Kindly

Speaking Heavenly words

Words that evoke feelings of a first spring dawn

Breathing life into my work

They wonder why my words havent taken off

Grown wings as they say

And been given a wind behind them

Carrying them off into eternity?

I tell them firstly thank you

But you dont know the ways of God i say

Do you believe in Past Lives?

Well i had a Bad one right before this one

They called me the Executioner

In a Planet similar to this

I did Bad things to Good people

And gloated about it

It was on my record

And before i dropped into this one

One of their family members appeared right when the doors of Fate closed

He whispered something in the Operators ear

And from the look on his face I knew my Fate was sealed

He tucked a hand behind his ear

A tell in these parts

A bad bad fate was to come to me

One worst than the Boulder

I would write and write

And the words would go to deaf ears

Until my hands fell off

The same hands i used to conduct

The executions

So it is written

So i will read.
