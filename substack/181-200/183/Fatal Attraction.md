Fatal Attraction

I remember the first girl I was attracted to.

I think I was 8.

Years later we reconnected.

It was kinda strange a baddie at 24 telling me about my Yugioh card obsession.

Perhaps love is real.

On its own Love is..

But Human connection is so much more.

It involves the stars aligning,

and galaxies forming,

and people fighting,

and wars ending,

and ice fields drifting,

and culture conforming.
