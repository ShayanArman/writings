Perhaps...

1. Who are your competitors? What is one thing they are doing better than you are.

2. Is there a real market for your product? How do you know?

3. When you tell your potential customers about your product, do they instantly get it? Is it a real pain point?

4. How easy is it for you to acquire your customers. Do they call you? Or do you call them?

5. How well known are you in the industry? If not, why?

6. Is it easy to copy what you've done? Could a new competitor do exactly what you've done?
