Random Wednesdays
escape the regular

This is a decree.

Sundays are a day of Rest.

To recognize that even God took a break.

And Wednesdays? They are meant to be Random.

Completely different than any other Wednesday.

You cannot eat what you’ve eaten.
You cannot do what you’ve done.

Last week I ended up at a strip club.

Last night (obviously a wednesday) we went to a Korean restaurant and learned about Chi-Mac.

In Uni I came up with it. That all Wednesdays shall be random.

Random Wednesdays.
